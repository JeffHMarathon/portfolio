Carving Corners on the
Tail of the Dragon

318 curves in 11 miles. US 129 through the Appalachian Mountains at Deals Gap — known as the Tail of the Dragon — is one of the most iconic driving roads in the world. Taking our Miata through those corners is pure, uncut joy.

The same way I approach a complex reconciliation — with focus, precision, and a willingness to embrace the challenge — is exactly how I like to drive. Every bend is a decision, and the reward is the ride itself.
