01.

About Me

Hello! I'm Jeff Hojka, President of Khojant LLC and the architect behind The Marathon System — a cloud-hosted office suite that has been empowering independent insurance agencies since 1982.

Over more than three decades, I've built deep expertise in insurance technology, delivering integrated Agency Management and Premium Financing solutions that help agents track customers, manage documents, generate additional revenue, and run their entire office from any device.

I'm passionate about translating complex business needs into elegant, long-lasting software. My work spans everything from cloud infrastructure on AWS and Windows Server domains to custom application development in Clarion, C/C++, C#, and JavaScript. One of my proudest achievements was migrating The Marathon System to a fully cloud-based platform using TSPlus, IPDriver, and NETTALK hosted on Windows HyperV.

Outside of work, I enjoy 3D printing projects (find me on Facebook as Slacker 3D), and my musical taste runs the full spectrum — from the Beatles and Boston to jazz, blues, and metal.

Agency Management handles customer tracking, documents, company statements, and bank accounts. This allowed agencies to run the full system from any browser, from anywhere, without any local software installation.
